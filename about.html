Introduction

Who am I?

I'm a 32 year-old Web Developer with two MS degrees in the fields of psychology and counseling. My goal is to lead others to Christ by my example. I am passionate about helping others. With web design and development, I am able to express my creativity and organizational skills.

Where I'm From

I was born and raised in the middle Tennessee town of Columbia, TN. After college, I started my first job as a youth minister in Glasgow, KY. After discovering my passion for counseling, I moved to Nashville, TN for my graduate degrees. For the last three years, I have lived in Humboldt, TN with my lovely wife.
